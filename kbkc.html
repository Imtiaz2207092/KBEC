About Us 💡

KBEC was founded in 2019 to help KUET students learn entrepreneurship, attend workshops, and build projects. We organize competitions, events, and community activities for makers and future leaders.

  • 🏆 Business case competitions
  • 📚 Workshops and seminars
  • 🤝 Mentorship and teamwork
